General Information

Suzie is a long-haired Chihuahua with white and light brown fur. She was born on January 16, 2009 in Washington and she is currently 8 years old.
My family had brought her home on March 19, 2009 when she was only 2 months and a few days old.
Suzie lives with a family of 4, with my mom, dad, older sister, and I.
My mom's co-worker had named her Suzie because she thought that the girly name would suit her.
At first, my sister and I wanted to name her "Snowflake" or "Snowball" because of her white fur color, but then she started growing brown fur.
