The Life of Suzie

This is a website dedicated to my dog, Suzie. Here you will find some facts about her and pictures and videos of her. From this website, you will be able to get to know her better.

General Information

Suzie is a long-haired Chihuahua with white and light brown fur. She was born on January 16, 2009 in Washington and she is currently 8 years old. My family
had brought her home on March 19, 2009 when she was only 2 months and a few days old. Suzie lives with a family of 4 with my mom, dad, older sister, and I. My
mom's co-worker had named her Suzie because she thought that the girly name would suit her. At first, my sister and I wanted to name her "Snowflake" or "Snowball"
because of her white fur color, but then she started growing brown fur.

This is a picture of Suzie when she was 6 years old.

Words that She Understands in English

The only words that Suzie understands in English is "shower" and "Suzie" because we do not speak to her in English that often.
She responds to the word "shower" by running away and her name by coming to the person.

Words and Phrases that She Understands in Vietnamese

  1. Go to sleep
  2. Take a shower
  3. Eat
  4. Go to the bathroom
  5. Go on a walk
  6. Haircut
  7. Cutting her nails (manipedi)
  8. Cage
  9. Kiss
  10. Mom, dad, and sister
  11. What is it?
  12. Who is it?

Suzie runs away after hearing some of these words because as you may know, some of these things are her dislikes.
While she will come closer to the person after hearing about some things that she does like.
