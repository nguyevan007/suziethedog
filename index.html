General Information

Suzie is a long-haired Chiahuahua with white and light brown fur. She was born on January 16, 2009 in Washington and she is currently 8 years old.
My family had brought her home on March 19 2009 when she was only a few months old.
My mom's co-worker had named her Suzie because she thought that the girly name would suit her. At first, my sister and I wanted to name her snowflake or snowball because of her white fur color, until she started growing brown fur.

My Favorite Music Genre

I prefer mostly all songs that are upbeat and alternative music that sets a calm mood. The upbeat music usually makes me more energetic and happy. The alternative music calms me down and makes me reflect on life.
